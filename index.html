The News Aggregator site offers the following features:

         Users can register and log in to personalize their experience.
        2. Dark/Light Mode:
         Toggle between dark and light themes for better readability.
        3. News Search:
         Search for news articles using keywords.
        4. User Preferences:
         Select preferred news categories to tailor content.
        5. Bookmarks:
         Save and manage favorite articles for easy access later.
